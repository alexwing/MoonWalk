MoonWalk 2.0
------------
Game for Google Cardboard VR, you can explore the lunar surface, shoot, jump and break ground.
Xbox One Controller

## Requirements

Unity2018.2.16f1


## Xbox One Controller

Left Stick – Walk

Btn A – Shoot and down terrrain

Btn B – Shoot and upper terrrain

Btn Y – Jump

Right Stick – Rotate camera

## Controls

Cursors – Walk

Space – Jump

Z – Shoot

X – Kill a enemy

C – Generates less enemies

V – Generates more enemies

## Only in Windows

O – Vr desactivated

P – Vr activated

Click Mouse – Shoot

Mouse – Move head

Ctrl + Mouse – Rotate head
